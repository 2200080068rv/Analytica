About Us

The Analytica is the prestigious club of K L University and focuses solely on working with the students on collaborative projects based on interest in data science and analytics. Our club gives a platform for students to do a project based on hands-on activity with different workshops that develop and refine skills so very pertinent to this new, data-driven world of today. Whether a fresh data lover or an experienced member, Analytica has all hands on deck to help and support you in the way possible. This is where we hold various hackathons, guest lectures, and team projects that further allow members to gain on-the-field practical experience and garner newer tools and networking prospects in the field.
